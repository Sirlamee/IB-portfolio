Interactive Text Painter

Hover over any letter to paint it black. The color will remain after you move your cursor away!
Welcome to the interactive text painter!
Move your cursor over any letter to paint it black.
Each letter you hover over will stay black permanently.
Create patterns and designs with your mouse movements.
This is a fun way to interact with text on a webpage!
Try painting entire words or just random letters.
The possibilities are endless with this simple effect.
